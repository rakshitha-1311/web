Strawberry Cheesecake Poke Cake



Preperation

1 Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2 Spray a 9x9-inch (23x23 cm) baking pan with cooking spray.
3 Take 5 of the graham crackers and arrange them in the pan, breaking some of them to fill in the gaps.
4 Crush the remaining graham cracker and set aside.
5 Pour the vanilla cake batter on the graham cracker base.
6 Bake for 25-30 minutes until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
7 Using the back of a wooden spoon, poke 4 rows of 4 holes in the cake, making 16 holes total.
8 In a large bowl, mash strawberries with a whisk until very fine.
9 Add cream cheese and condensed milk, whisking until there are no large lumps.
10 Add the milk, stirring until smooth.
11 Pour the mixture over the cake and set until the cake has absorbed as much of the mixture as it can. Some of the mixture will still stay on top, which is okay.
12 Spread the whipped topping evenly on top.
13 Chill the cake anywhere from 3 hours to overnight.
14 Slice, and serve with strawberries and the reserved graham cracker crumbs.
15 Enjoy!

Ingredients

. cooking spray
. 6 graham crackers, whole
. ½ box vanilla cake mix, prepared according to package
. 1 lb strawberry, with tops removed
. 8 oz cream cheese, softened, 1 block
. 14 oz sweetened condensed milk, 1 can
. 1 cup milk
. 8 oz whipped topping
. strawberry, for garnish
