Marshmallow Kiss Cookies



Preperation

1 Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2 Unwrap Hershey’s Kisses. Set aside for later.
3 Lightly dampen marshmallows by patting down with a wet paper towel, or dipping them into a small bowl of water. Coat well in sprinkles. Set aside to dry.
4 Combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
5 Mix together butter and sugar until creamy. Add egg, vanilla, and milk. Mix until well combined.
6 Add flour mixture and combine well.
7 Scoop tablespoon-sized amounts onto a parchment-lined cookie sheet.
8 Bake for 10-12 minutes.
9 Make a small indention. Place a marshmallow in the divot.
10 Bake for 2 - 2½ minutes.
11 Remove from oven and press Hershey’s Kisses in the center of the marshmallow.
12 Let cool.
13 Enjoy!

Ingredients

. 24 hershey's kiss
. ½ cup sprinkles
. water
. 24 large marshmallows
. 2 cups flour
. ½ teaspoon baking soda
. ½ teaspoon salt
. ½ cup butter, softened
. 1 cup sugar
. 1 large egg
. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
. ½ cup milk
